The Food Bank @ St Mary's

In downtown Seattle, the Pike Market Senior Center strives to make each day better and safer for older adults; the Pike Market Food Bank serves people of all ages who face hunger. Together, they provide services, activities, and support that lead to more self sufficient and fulfilling lives.
